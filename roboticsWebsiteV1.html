About the Mission

Commander Nate and TechGhost are launching Project EVO — a robotics initiative exploring the boundaries of automation, AI, and underwater exploration. It all begins with Spider V1, an eight-legged prototype built for adaptability and resilience, destined for land, sea, and beyond.

Phase Zero: “Ascent from the Abyss — For Home and Horizon.” We rise from darkness to create light. Learn more in our mission log.

Prototype Spotlight: Spider V1

  • 24 DOF using MG90S servos for advanced movement
  • Hollow 3D-printed legs for internal wiring and modularity
  • Waterproofing strategies to prepare for marine deployment
  • Upgrade-ready from PLA to PETG or ABS
